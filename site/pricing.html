Pricing questions we've heard
What counts as an "AI system"?
One distinct model deployed for one purpose. A single PPE-detection model served to 50 construction sites is one system. A separate hazard-classification model is a second system. If you fine-tune the same base model for a new use case, that's a new system.
Why no free tier?
A paid pilot is a two-way commitment. We invest in onboarding you; you invest in actually integrating and using the product. Free users ghost; paying customers give feedback. If $2,500 is genuinely blocking, ask us — budget shouldn't be the only reason not to start.
I'm a US-only company. Do I need this?
If any of your customers deploy your AI system to users in the EU, the Act applies to you as a provider. Most construction-AI vendors selling into Europe already fall under it — even if your HQ is in San Francisco.
Where does my data live?
By default, inference metadata lives in a local SQLite database on your infrastructure. We never receive raw inputs or outputs. For Enterprise, we support pipelining evidence logs to your own SIEM or data warehouse.
What if I have 6 systems — do I need Scale?
No. The Team limit is soft. If you're at 5–7 systems and growing, start on Team and we'll move you to Scale when you hit 10. We'd rather have you on the right tier than stall the integration.
Multi-year discount?
10% off Team for 2 years, 15% off for 3 years. Scale gets 15% / 20% on the same terms. Written into the order form.
